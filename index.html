ABOUT

Hello, there. My name is Atlas and I'm a full stack developer!

I have been working in this field for roughly one year as of 2021 but my career began as a Data Analyst and over the years I progressed into the role of Data Scientist. I decided to step across the boundary into software development because I'm a creative at heart...

But don't misunderstand, data is fun! I still enjoy writing SQL queries and developing performance frameworks and methodologies, but creating data visualizations wasn't creative enough for me. I wanted to be able to ship a product and say 'Hey, I helped code that!'

As such, I began developing my full stack skills from 2020 onwards and took my first payment for freelance work within the same year. Since then I've been having a great time working with all kinds of clients on all sorts of projects.

For my next step I'm looking to work abroad as a full stack developer, ideally in Japan, as it was my residence nearly 10 years ago and I'd like to return! If you're interested in hiring me please consider this website and my attached portfolio work and reach out to me!
